The Common Breaks

Where most companies lose signal

01

GA4 Installed, Nothing Tracking

The script is there but events are misfiring, duplicated, or never reaching GA4. You have data but none of it is trustworthy.

02

No Tag Management Layer

Tracking code is hardcoded in the site. Every change requires a developer. Events are inconsistent across pages.

03

Dark Traffic & Misattribution

UTM parameters are missing or inconsistent. Direct traffic is inflated. Campaign ROI is unmeasurable.

04

Zero Conversion Tracking

Form submissions, purchases, phone calls — none are connected back to the ad or campaign that drove them.

05

Client-Side Only (Ad Blockers Win)

Browser tracking loses 20–40% of conversions to ad blockers and ITP. Server-side tagging is the fix.

06

CRM & Ad Platform Disconnect

Your Salesforce deals have no link to the Google Ads campaign that started them. Offline conversion import is broken or absent.
